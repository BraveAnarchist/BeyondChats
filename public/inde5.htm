    Consistently voted one of the best restaurants in Northeast Florida, bb’s restaurant and bar never cease to please and surprise the palate of discerning diners, locals, and visitors alike. We are conveniently located in Jacksonville’s South Bank Area, mere minutes from Historic San Marco and the downtown business district. Our award-winning wine list, creative daily specials, and house-made desserts will have you return for more.
